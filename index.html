Build, Educate and Skate

Who are we?

UNITED BY SKATEBOARDING is an None Governmental Organization (NGO). It was established in 202_ by Namibian street skateboarders, who skated (shredded) the rough Namibian terrain for many years... shredding pavements, basketball courts, malls, schools, churches, roads and hills (bombing hills in Windhoek)... Our experience however rad, showed us first hand how difficult it is to be a skateboarder in Namibia, it also showed us how skateboarding can be used as a vehicle for positive transformation within our local communities. We aim to build skateparks across the country especially in underprivileged communities that can benefit from skateboarding discipline and culture, thus we seek your support, collaboration and assistance to make our NGO project a success.
